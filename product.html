51V 105AH Thru-Hole LiFePO4 Battery

$1,980.00

An Eco Battery exclusive and top seller, the 51V 105Ah Thru-Hole battery features a unique, patented design that allows for simple installation using a center mount rod. It provides a straightforward fit for Club Car carts using the stock J-hook and, with a simple bracket, is compatible with Yamaha, EZGO, ICON, and more. This upgrade significantly boosts range and eliminates the maintenance hassle associated with lead-acid batteries.
