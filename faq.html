BMS stands for Battery Management System. It acts as a bridge between the battery and its users, ensuring the safety and longevity of the battery. The primary role of the BMS is to protect the battery cells from damage caused by factors like over-voltage, under-voltage, overcurrent, high temperatures, or external short circuits. If unsafe operating conditions arise, the BMS automatically shuts off the battery to prevent harm. All Trontek batteries come equipped with a built-in BMS to safeguard against such risks. Specifically, the BMS in our forklift batteries features a cutting-edge design with advanced capabilities, including: Remote Monitoring with OTA (Over-the-Air Updates) Thermal Management Multiple Protection Mechanisms like Low Voltage Protection, Over Voltage Protection, and Short Circuit Protection. This innovative system ensures maximum safety and efficiency for your lithium batteries.

Trontek batteries are designed to last around 3,500 life cycles. The battery's design life is approximately 10 years, and we provide a 5-year warranty. While Trontek LiFePO4 Batteries may have a higher initial cost, the upgrade can save you up to 70% on battery expenses over a 5-year period.

Please note that Trontek lithium batteries work most effectively with the original Trontek charger. Keep in mind: if you continue using your lead-acid battery charger, it will not be compatible with our lithium batteries. Additionally, we cannot guarantee optimal performance or safety when using other chargers. Our technicians strongly recommend using the original Trontek charger for the best results.
